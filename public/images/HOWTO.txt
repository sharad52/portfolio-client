# Add Your Images Here

## What to Add:

### Required Images (Recommended):
- `profile.jpg` - Your professional photo (800x800px)
- `hero-background.jpg` - Hero section background (1920x1080px)

### Optional Images:
- Project screenshots in `/projects/` folder
- Blog post covers in `/blog/` folder
- Technology icons in `/tech-icons/` folder
- Company logos in `/company-logos/` folder

## Quick Tips:

1. **Keep file sizes small** - Compress images before uploading
2. **Use descriptive names** - `ecommerce-project-cover.jpg` not `img123.jpg`
3. **Consistent formats** - JPG for photos, PNG for graphics, SVG for icons
4. **Optimize before upload** - Use TinyPNG or similar tools

## Image Sources:

If you don't have images yet:
- **Unsplash** (https://unsplash.com) - Free high-quality photos
- **Pexels** (https://pexels.com) - Free stock photos
- **UI Faces** (https://uifaces.co) - Profile pictures
- **Simple Icons** (https://simpleicons.org) - Technology logos

Your images will automatically be available at `/images/filename.jpg` in your code!
